FAQ

How do I create a timer?

Tap + on the home screen. Choose "Sequenz Builder" to build manually, or "Smart Creator" to let AI create one for you.

How do I sync with Apple Watch?

Go to Settings > Sync to Apple Watch and enable it. Your timers sync automatically.

How do I restore my purchase?

Settings > Premium > "Restore Purchases". Use the same Apple ID as the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > SQNZ > Cancel.

How do I delete my data?

Settings > Data & Privacy. Delete timers, history, AI data, or everything at once.

Voice commands don't work?

Make sure microphone access is granted in iOS Settings > SQNZ > Microphone.
