SQNZ · Sequenz

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

SQNZ is an interval timer app for iOS and Apple Watch. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Stored On Your Device

  • Timer configurations — Your created timers, flows, and settings
  • Workout statistics — Session history, streaks, and duration data
  • Heart rate data — If you grant HealthKit access, heart rate is read during workouts and stored locally
  • Notes — Any personal notes you add to timers
  • Preferences — App settings, language, sound preferences

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, address)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not store any data on external servers

Voice Commands

When voice commands are enabled, the microphone is active during workouts. All audio is processed on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

AI Timer Creator

If you use the AI Timer Creator (requires subscription), your text input is sent to an AI service to generate timer configurations. No personal data beyond the workout description you type is transmitted.

Apple HealthKit

The App can read heart rate data from Apple Health during workouts. This data is used solely to display your heart rate and calculate workout statistics. Health data is never shared with third parties or transmitted off your device.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your timer configurations are synced via your personal iCloud account. This data is protected by Apple's iCloud security and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time through Settings > Data & Privacy in the App. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@sqnz-app.com
