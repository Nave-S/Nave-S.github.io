ShutterLife

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

ShutterLife is a camera shutter count reader for photographers, available for iOS and macOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data ShutterLife collects, uses, and shares — which is none.

Data Collection

ShutterLife does not collect any data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No photos or image files are uploaded or transmitted
  • No camera data (model, serial number, shutter count) leaves your device
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are collected
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

Data Processing

All image file processing (EXIF parsing, shutter count extraction) happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

Data Stored On Your Device

  • Shutter count readings — Camera model, shutter count, and date of each reading
  • Reading history — Previous readings stored locally for tracking camera wear over time

This data is only accessible by the ShutterLife app, never transmitted to any external service, and deleted when you uninstall the app. You can also delete all data at any time from within the app (History > Delete All).

Internet Connection

ShutterLife does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

ShutterLife does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Camera Access (macOS)

On macOS, ShutterLife can connect to USB cameras to read shutter count data directly. This connection is local and no camera data is transmitted externally.

Children's Privacy

ShutterLife does not collect any data from any user, including children.

Your Rights

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data to request, modify, or delete on our end. All data remains on your device under your full control.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at shutterlife.support@gmail.com
