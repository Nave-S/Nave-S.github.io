Description

ShutterLife is a camera shutter count reader for photographers. It reads EXIF data from camera images (RAW and JPEG) to display the number of shutter actuations and estimate remaining shutter life. Available for iOS and macOS.

Use of the App

  • The app is intended for personal and professional use by photographers
  • You may import your own camera images to read shutter count data

Accuracy Disclaimer

Shutter count readings are extracted from image EXIF metadata and camera manufacturer data. While we strive for accuracy, readings may vary depending on camera model, firmware version, and image format. Shutter life estimates are based on manufacturer-rated shutter durability and are approximations only. ShutterLife does not guarantee the accuracy of any reading or estimate.

No Data Collection

ShutterLife operates entirely offline. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers. All processing happens on your device.
